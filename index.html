Who are we?

Welcome to ABC Travel Co., your dedicated companion for exploring the breathtaking hill stations of India.

We are a specialized travel company committed to crafting unique and unforgettable journeys into the country's most serene and majestic mountainous regions.

From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the lush green landscapes of the Western Ghats, our mission is to connect travelers with the natural beauty and rich cultural heritage of India's highlands.

ABC Travel Co. offers carefully curated packages that promise not just a trip, but an immersive experience, ensuring every escape to the mountains is seamless, memorable, and filled with wonder.

Some useful Travel Terminologies

Round Trip:
A journey to a destination and its subsequent return journey.
Stopover:
A longer stay(usually more than 24 hours) in a city during a journey.
ETA:
Short abbreviation for Estimated Time of Arrival.
